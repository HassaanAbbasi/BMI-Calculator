BMI Calculator

Body mass index (BMI) is a measure of body fat that is calculated using the height and weight of a person. Essentially, it lets you know if your weight is in the "healthy range".

Knowing your BMI is useful, as it can tell you when you may be at risk for a number of health conditions. For example, a BMI greater than 30 means that you qualify as an obese person, and are at a greater chance of having:

  • High blood pressure
  • Body pain
  • Heart disease
  • Type 2 Diabetes
